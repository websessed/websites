My name is Manuwa I am an indie Afropop artist, a songwriter and a
Guitarist based in Lagos. I love writing and performing songs and I
have been doing it for over 8 years. I was a runner up in the Reality TV
show, Nigerian Idol in 2015 and I Opened for Adekunle Gold in his last
"Catch me if you can" Concert at Eko hotels in 2022.

One unique attribute about Manuwa is his versatility in writing music
across multiple genres and connecting on a deeper level with his fans through his lyrics.
His new single "Where u dey" was released on Sept 22nd, 2023. And this is a taste of great things
to come. For coming events, He plays at 360 signature at 1st ugbor benin city every thursday...
